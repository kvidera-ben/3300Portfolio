What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files stored on your device when you visit a website. They are used to remember your preferences and improve your experience.

How I Use Cookies

This site uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • To analyze site traffic using tools like Google Analytics
  • To remember your theme or layout preferences (if applicable)
  • To ensure site functionality and security

Managing Cookies

You can choose to disable or delete cookies in your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may impact how the site functions.
